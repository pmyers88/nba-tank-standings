NBA Tank Standings

NBA Tank Standings provides information about the latest standings in the NBA draft lottery. You can find out which pick(s) your favorite team has, including if your team has received any picks via trade.

You can also try simulating the draft lottery. Maybe your team will get the #1 pick!

Example Interactions

Here are some things you can try with the NBA Tank Rankings Skill:

  • Alexa, ask NBA Tank Standings what are the latest standings
  • Alexa, ask NBA Tank Standings what are the top 7 teams
  • Alexa, ask NBA Tank Standings info about the 76ers
  • Alexa, ask NBA Tank Standings to simulate the lottery
