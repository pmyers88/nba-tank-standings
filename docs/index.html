NBA Tank Standings

NBA Tank Standings is an Amazon Echo skill that provides information about the latest standings in the NBA draft lottery. You can find out which pick(s) your favorite team has, including if your team has received any picks via trade.

You can also try simulating the draft lottery. Maybe your team will get the #1 pick!

Install

Say "Alexa, enable NBA Tank Standings" or search for NBA Tank Standings in the skills section of your Alexa app.

Demo

Check out a demonstration of the app in action!

Example Interactions

In case you can't view the demo right now, here are some things you can try with the NBA Tank Standings skill:

  • Alexa, ask NBA Tank Standings to simulate the lottery
  • Alexa, ask NBA Tank Standings what are the latest standings
  • Alexa, ask NBA Tank Standings what are the top 10 teams
  • Alexa, ask NBA Tank Standings info about the 76ers
